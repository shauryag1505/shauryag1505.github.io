FAQs: Smart Screen Time, Healthier Mind

Get answers to the most pressing questions about screen time, mental well-being, and achieving a balanced lifestyle.

How does excessive screen time affect mental health?

Too much screen time increases stress, anxiety, and disrupts sleep by overstimulating the brain and reducing real-world interactions.

Can screen time really cause physical health problems?

Yes! Eye strain, poor posture, headaches, and sleep disturbances are common effects of prolonged screen exposure.

How much screen time is actually healthy for students?

Experts recommend less than 2 hours of recreational screen time daily, with breaks and mindful usage for better focus and well-being.

What are the best ways to balance screen time and studies?

Follow the 20-20-20 rule, set screen-free zones, use focus timers, and prioritize real-world activities for a healthier routine.
