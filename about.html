Our Story

Social Dilemma: Catering Teenagers from High School to help overcome Stress-Anxiety and Manage Screentime.

Geetanjali Dhull

Head of Well Being Counsellors

As a Head of Well-Being, Ms. Geetanjali brings years of experience in guiding teenagers toward better mental and emotional health. As an expert in adolescent psychology, she has worked closely with high school students, helping them navigate the growing challenges of stress, anxiety, and digital overload. In today’s fast-paced world, excessive screen time has become a major concern, significantly impacting the mental well-being, emotional stability, and physical health of teenagers. Witnessing firsthand the rising cases of stress, anxiety, and sleep disturbances among students, Ms. Dhull felt a deep responsibility to address this issue. This website is her initiative to create awareness, provide guidance, and offer practical solutions to help students take control of their digital habits.

Our Journey

The Beginning: A Silent Takeover
It starts small—just a quick check of messages, a scroll through social media, a five-minute YouTube break. Before you know it, hours slip away, swallowed by endless notifications, bright screens, and algorithm-driven distractions. What once felt like entertainment and connection slowly turns into exhaustion, stress, and anxiety. For many high school students, this is no longer just a habit—it’s a way of life.
A world where screens dictate emotions, where likes and comments decide self-worth, and where the pressure to stay connected is overwhelming. The cost? Mental fatigue, loss of focus, declining health, and a growing sense of isolation.

The Realization: When Connection Becomes Disconnection
As a Head of Well-Being Counsellor in an international school, Geetanjali Dhull has seen this firsthand. Students walk into her office, burdened with stress, struggling with anxiety, trapped in a cycle they can’t seem to break."I just don’t have time anymore." "I can’t concentrate." "I don’t know why I feel so anxious."
The culprit? Uncontrolled screen time.
What was once a tool for learning and entertainment has become an invisible chain—limiting real-world experiences, damaging mental health, and disrupting the delicate balance of life.

The Turning Point: A Mission for Change
 Geetanjali knew this couldn’t continue. Students needed awareness, guidance, and a way to regain control. That’s why she created this platform—a space where teenagers, parents, and educators can understand the real impact of excessive screen time and learn how to break free.
→ The Science Behind Screen Time & Mental Health –
Discover how digital overload leads to stress, anxiety, and emotional instability.
→ The Hidden Cost on Physical Health –
Explore how too much screen exposure affects sleep, posture, eyesight, and overall well-being.
→ Building Healthier Digital Habits –
Learn simple, powerful strategies to balance screen time without feeling disconnected.
→ Mastering Time Management –
Get age-specific schedules to create a lifestyle that balances academics, social life, and personal growth.

The Transformation: A Balanced, Healthier You
 This is not about quitting screens—it’s about using them wisely. It’s about taking back control, setting boundaries, and making time for what truly matters:
✨ Real conversations.
✨ Quality sleep.
✨ Mindful productivity.
✨ A stress-free mind.
This journey is about you—learning to live in harmony with technology, rather than being consumed by it.
Welcome to Social Dilemma—where awareness meets action, and balance becomes reality.
