Tackling Stress & Managing Time in a Digital Age

With screentime on the rise, teenagers today face growing stress and anxiety from packed schedules and constant digital distractions. This page offers age-appropriate balanced daily schedule to balance your daily routine, reduce overwhelm, and reclaim your calm amidst the storm.

Age Group: 5-10 Years

Time Activity
7:00 AM - 8:00 AM Wake up, breakfast, morning hygiene
8:00 AM - 12:00 PM School or learning activities (minimal screen use)
12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Lunch and relaxation
1:00 PM - 3:00 PM Outdoor play or creative activities (no screens)
3:00 PM - 4:00 PM Screen time (educational games/TV, 1 hour max)
4:00 PM - 6:00 PM Homework or reading (physical books)
6:00 PM - 7:00 PM Dinner and family time
7:00 PM - 8:00 PM Wind down (no screens, storytelling or board games)
8:00 PM Bedtime

Age Group: 10-15 Years

Time Activity
6:30 AM - 7:30 AM Wake up, breakfast, morning routine
7:30 AM - 2:00 PM School (some screen use for learning)
2:00 PM - 3:00 PM Lunch and short break
3:00 PM - 5:00 PM Homework/study (1 hour screen time max)
5:00 PM - 6:30 PM Physical activity (sports, outdoor play)
6:30 PM - 8:00 PM Dinner and screen time (after dinner study, 1.5 hours max)
8:00 PM - 9:00 PM Relaxation (reading, music, no screens)
9:00 PM Bedtime

Age Group: 15-20 Years

Time Activity
6:00 AM - 7:00 AM Wake up, breakfast, morning routine
7:00 AM - 3:00 PM School/college (screen use as needed for studies)
3:00 PM - 4:00 PM Lunch and downtime
4:00 PM - 6:00 PM Study/homework (1.5 hours screen time max)
6:00 PM - 7:30 PM Exercise or socializing (no screens)
7:30 PM - 9:30 PM Screen time (entertainment/social media, 2 hours max)
9:30 PM - 10:00 PM Wind down (no screens, meditation or reading)
10:00 PM Bedtime
